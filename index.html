About

I was born in Sweden in July 1990 in a medium-sized city called Helsingborg. As a kid my biggest passions were playing football and drawing scenes from action movies my parents let me watch far too young. My interests changed when I started high school, which had a focus on finance and business. Perhaps I should become a stock broker on Wall Street? While this was the route of many of my friends, I had rekindled my relationship with movies, as well as writing, and decided to instead move to Hollywood and study screenwriting. It was a struggle, but I managed to sell an original screenplay to Warner Brothers, who ultimately decided not to produce it. During the years as an aspiring writer, I held down many different jobs; from social media manager at a Berlin startup, to customer service representative for Apple (not to mention Christmas tree salesman during a winter in Manhattan), while studying for a BA in philosophy in the early mornings and evenings. Tired of the financial unpredictability of the writing life, but still eager for something creative, as well as another big challenge, in the spring of 2024 I decided to start learning how to code. It’s been an amazing adventure, and I’m still learning every day…
